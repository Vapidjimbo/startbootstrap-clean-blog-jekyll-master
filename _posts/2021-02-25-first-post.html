Cats? Who Cares?

Meow Meow Meow, feed me. -Mr.Kittens .

Humans care, and a lot. Cats have been with us for longer than recorded history. There are pictures of them in ancient Egyptian ruins and evidence of their domestication all over the world from ancient times. They only come in second to dogs.

There are many reasons why a partnership has formed between the two of us. Cats got food, and we got pest control. They also make good, if not annoying companions. This is what this blog is all about, be sure to check back daily for the newest content!
